About the ASCVD Calculator

The Pooled Cohort Equations (PCE) estimate the 10-year risk of a first atherosclerotic cardiovascular disease (ASCVD) event — defined as non-fatal myocardial infarction, coronary heart disease death, or fatal or non-fatal stroke.

This calculator is intended for patients aged 40–79 years who have not previously been diagnosed with cardiovascular disease. It was derived and validated specifically for White and African American adults.
