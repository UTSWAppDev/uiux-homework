About the PREVENT Calculator

The Predicting Risk of Cardiovascular Disease EVENTs (PREVENT) equations were published by the American Heart Association in 2023. PREVENT estimates the 10-year risk of a total CVD event — including coronary heart disease and stroke — in adults aged 30–79 years.

Key improvements over the older Pooled Cohort Equations:

  • Race-free: does not use race as a variable, making it appropriate for all patients.
  • Broader age range: applies to adults aged 30–79 (PCE covers only 40–79).
  • Includes kidney function: eGFR is an independent cardiovascular risk factor.
